Overview
I distinctly remember the moment I found out Santa wasn't real. In an innocent conversation, I began to ask my mother questions about Santa's magic and how it worked. Submitting to my intense questioning, she eventually admitted that there was no such thing as magic. It took me until my freshman year of high school to realize she was wrong. After years of confusion, I applied to Hogwarts School of Magic where I began my study of accounting. I currently attend Brigham Young University in hopes to reintegrate back into normal society where I can gradually introduce magic back into the world of business.
